2.Description about the Dashboard 

This dashboard analyzes stock prices, revenue, net income, trading volume, and profit margins of NVIDIA, AMD, and Intel over multiple years. It enables users to compare company performance and identify financial trends using interactive visualizations.

4.Data Description

The dataset contains historical financial and stock market data for NVIDIA, AMD, and Intel. It includes attributes such as revenue, net income, net margin, stock closing prices, and trading volume across multiple years, quarters, months, and days.

5.Key Dashboard Features

- KPI cards showing total revenue, net income, and gross margin  
- Year-wise revenue growth analysis for NVIDIA  
- Net margin analysis over time  
- Stock price comparison across companies  
- Company-wise comparison of revenue and net income  
- Trading volume analysis over time  
- Interactive filters and drill-down capabilities  

6.Insights Generated
   
- NVIDIA shows strong revenue growth from 2020 to 2024  
- Intel leads in overall revenue and net income compared to AMD and NVIDIA  
- NVIDIA demonstrates higher profit margins in recent years  
- Stock prices and trading volumes vary significantly across companies and time periods  
- Financial performance trends can be clearly observed using year-over-year comparisons
